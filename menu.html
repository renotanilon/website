About Our Municipality

Welcome to Kolambugan

Kolambugan is a progressive 3rd class municipality in the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. Located along the coastal area of Panguil Bay, it is known for its rich cultural heritage, natural resources, and warm, friendly people.

The municipality was founded in 1936 and has since developed into a vibrant community with a growing economy focused on agriculture, fishing, and trade. Kolambugan is strategically positioned, making it an important gateway to other municipalities and provinces in Mindanao.

Our municipality spans approximately 134.55 square kilometers and is home to over 30,000 residents spread across 26 barangays. The people of Kolambugan are known for their hospitality, strong community ties, and dedication to preserving their cultural traditions while embracing progress.

Kolambugan is blessed with natural resources including fertile agricultural lands, rich marine resources from Panguil Bay, and scenic landscapes that attract visitors. The local economy is diversified with thriving sectors in agriculture, fisheries, small-scale industries, and an emerging tourism industry.

With its beautiful beaches, historical sites, and flourishing local industries, Kolambugan offers a unique blend of traditional and modern Filipino culture. Our municipality is committed to sustainable development, environmental protection, and improving the quality of life for all residents.

Development Projects

January 2025

Public Market Renovation

Complete renovation and modernization of the municipal public market to provide better facilities for vendors and customers. The project includes improved stalls, proper drainage systems, and better waste management facilities.

November 2024

Coastal Road Expansion

Expansion of the coastal road connecting major barangays to improve transportation and boost tourism in the area. This project has significantly reduced travel time between coastal communities.

August 2024

Healthcare Center Establishment

Construction of a modern healthcare center with improved facilities to provide better medical services to residents. The center is equipped with new medical equipment and staffed by qualified healthcare professionals.

March 2024

Clean Water System

Implementation of a comprehensive clean water system reaching remote barangays to ensure access to safe drinking water for all residents. This project has significantly reduced waterborne diseases in the municipality.
